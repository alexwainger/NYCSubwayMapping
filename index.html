A Week in the Life of 53,364 NYC Subways

Inspired by Chris Whong's NYC Taxis: A Day in the Life project, this visualization uses GTFS data from the MTA to visualize the New York City subway system.

Note: The MTA's dataset is missing the geographic data for certain lines, such as the 1, G, and W trains. These trains will not appear on the map, but their schedules are still taken into account in calculating wait times.
